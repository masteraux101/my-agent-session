### Insights:
- **Qiskit** remains the dominant force with the highest community engagement and most frequent updates.
- **Cirq** maintains a dedicated following, particularly in research and chemistry.
- **Q#** has seen a resurgence in interest since the release of the "Modern QDK," which removed the heavy .NET dependency, making it much more accessible to Python developers.
