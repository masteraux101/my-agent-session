| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |

| **Documentation** | Best-in-class, interactive. | Technical and research-oriented. | Good, heavily integrated with VS Code. |
| **Local Simulation** | Robust (Aer/Primitives). | Fast (Simulators). | Extremely fast (Native binary). |
| **Error Rate (Scripts)** | 0% (Stable API). | 0% (Stable API). | 5% (Environment setup overhead). |
